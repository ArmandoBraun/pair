Plataform

Taxi Network Management System

PAIR’s platform captures and analyzes the data coming from the taxi operation, allowing the Transport Authority to visualize and manage the fleet in real time. This way, regulators are able to understand and optimize operations, detect anomalies and complement the overall urban transportation system.

Platform
Features

Visualization

  • Map: Ongoing trips, historic data about trip duration, trip distance, time, cost.
  • Driver Rating
  • Fleet Status: Licences, coming expirations, renewals.

Smart Analytics

  • High-Demand Zones: identify the most active areas in the city and the trips between them.
  • Fleet Optimization data insights about how efficiently taxis are utilized.
  • Carbon Footprint: Estimate the key metrics such as energy consumption, energy intensity (energy per dollar of fare income), and carbon emissions.

Real-Time fleet management

  • Taxi Fare Management (static and dynamic): Data insights to support regulators’ fare review process including average trip distance distribution and average vacant distance.
  • Dispatch.
  • Messaging Module: High demand zone alerts, license expiring, traffic alerts.

Smart Hub

  • Optimize the supply and demand at the hub
  • Track the trajectory of taxis inside the hub
  • Ride-sharing from hubs trip management
